About

Professionalism you can always count on

Established in 1982, Fuji Dental Lab has built a prestigious reputation as “The Big ‘Little’ Lab.”

Combining the skills, technology and quality of a big lab while maintaining the small lab customer service and spirit, Fuji takes great pride in building relationships with doctors and providing them with the highest quality dental products and services on the market.

Fuji Dental Lab is a full service lab that offers custom fixed, removable and implant dental products and services. These include dental crowns, dental bridges, veneers, dentures, partial dentures and dental implants. Fuji also offers screw-retained fixed hybrid dentures such as All-on-4® and Conus. Fuji is proud to follow the Pankey and Dawson practices in all of our quality restorations, and is one of the first labs in the country to establish it's very own In-House Milling Center.

The needs of the dentists and patients are always top priority. Each member of the Fuji Dental Lab team is committed to providing a personal touch and exceptional service for each and every Fuji dentist. Each case is treated as if it were the only one! The Fuji Dental Lab team continues to strive toward their goal of being the best dental laboratory in the industry today.

Each team member is committed to providing a personal touch and exceptional service for each of our dentist-clients, and to handling each dental case as if it were our only case! We are always willing and able to meet the needs of each of our dentists and will continue to strive to be the very best dental laboratory in our industry today.

Services

Time is Money


At Fuji Dental Laboratory, we understand that your time is valuable.

With our in house Milling Center and 3D model creation, we can help your office streamline chair-time and patient scheduling with shorter turnaround times on your cases!

Fuji Dental Lab has accepted digital impressions for many years, but in the past we would have to wait days for third party vendors to send the models to us.

To improve the quality and efficiency of our production, we have invested in Carbon 3D printers to create the models for our dentists who utilize intraoral scanners. We also offer single-unit posterior crowns with a “model free” option for our doctors who don’t want models for single unit posterior cases. We accept digital impressions for most open-platform intraoral scanners that use .STL export files.
